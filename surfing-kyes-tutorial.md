# Basic surfing keys tutorial Cheat Sheet

## Character Motions

- **`h`** - switch to the left tab.
- **`l`** - switch to the right tab.

- **`j`** - scroll down slowly.
- **`k`** - scroll up slowly.
- **`d`** - scroll down faster.
- **`e`** - scroll up faster.
- **`d`** - Move up one line.
- **`gg`** - Go top of the file.
- **`G`** - Go bottom of the file.
- **`u`** - undo changes.
